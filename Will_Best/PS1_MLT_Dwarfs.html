Photometry and Proper Motions of M, L, and T Dwarfs from the
Pan-STARRS1 3pi Survey

 

November 8, 2017 (latest release)


This page contains electronic versions of tables of photometry and proper motions for 9,888 M, L, and T dwarfs observed by the
Pan-STARRS1 3pi Survey (PS1), presented in Best et al. 2018, ApJS, 234, 1


If you use the data from these tables, please:

(1) cite original sources for non-PS1 data (all references provided in the tables);

(2) acknowledge this catalog by citing Best et al. (2018).

  1. (3)If you use the parallaxes in these tables, please also cite the Database of Ultracool Parallaxes maintained by Trent Dupuy; Dupuy & Liu (2012) (for field objects and binaries); Dupuy, & Kraus (2013) (for late-T and Y dwarfs); and Liu, Dupuy, & Allers (2016) (for young objects and companions).


All tables are in ASCII format.


The table headers contain column descriptions, footnotes, and references as cited in the paper.



Table of photometry: ps1_mlt_phot.txt

HISTORY

- Updated version of PS1 astrometry (PV3.3), reducing proper motion errors.

- Added 2MASS and WISE confusion/contamination flags.
