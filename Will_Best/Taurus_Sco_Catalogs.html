Proper Motions of Low-Mass Members of Taurus and Upper Scorpius

 

Artist's rendition by Dr. Robert Hurt of the Infrared Processing and Analysis Center.

January 31, 2017


This page contains electronic versions of tables of photometry and proper motions for low-mass (spectral types >~M3) members of the Taurus and star-forming regions and the Upper Scorpius association presented in appendices of Best et al. (2017, ApJ, 837, 95).


If you use the data from these tables, please cite Best et al. (2017).


Both tables are in ASCII format.


The table headers contain column descriptions and footnotes.
